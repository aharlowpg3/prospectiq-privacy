# ProspectIQ Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 16, 2026**

## Overview
ProspectIQ is a private recruiting board app for college coaches. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data We Collect
ProspectIQ stores the following data that coaches voluntarily enter:
- Prospect names
- Prospect phone numbers
- Prospect school and team information
- Scouting notes and evaluations

## How We Use Your Data
All data entered into ProspectIQ is used solely to power the app's recruiting board features. We do not sell, share, or distribute your data to any third parties.

## Data Storage
Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase Firestore. Data is accessible only to coaches who know your private board code.

## Data Deletion
To delete your data, contact us at the email below and we will remove it within 30 days.

## Contact
For privacy questions: prospectiq.app@gmail.com

## Changes
We may update this policy occasionally. Continued use of the app means you accept any changes.
